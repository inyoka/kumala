Children's Garden (Playgroup & TK)


Age Requirements

Calculated at beginning of the school year

    Toddler : Minimum age is 18 months
    PG : Jan – Dec 2014
    K1 : Jan – Dec 2013
    K2 : Jan – Dec 2012

Documantation

Complete the admission form and include:

    A copy of birth certificate (akte kelahiran)
    A copy of family register (kartu keluarga)
    3 recent colour photographs (pasfoto), sized 3X4
    2 recent colour photographs (pasfoto), sized 2X3
    1 copy of the childs immunization book
    Booking fee payment

Participate in the selection/observation class for 1-2 sessions, according to the set schedule.
This selection is based on attitude, adaptability, social interaction, listening skill and speaking skill.
