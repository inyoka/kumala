WSC (World Scholar's Cup) is a global celebration of learning and leadership. With more than 15000 participants from across 50 countries, this event brings students of diverse backgrounds together to explore new and interesting subjects. Chandra Kumala School proudly hosting Medan Round again in 2020. The two day event with theme [ A WORLD RENEWED ] will be done on the 27th to 28th February 2020.

Four activities were created to offer something more than just traditional academic competitions and to motivate students not to just demonstrate their existing strength but also to discover new ones.

There are four competitions in regional round to select which are :

  1. The Scholar's Challenge
  2. Collaborative Writing
  3. The Team Debate
  4. The Scholar's Bowl
Deadline for registration is 14th February 2020. Check out the link to WSC's website: Scholars Cup - Medan Round
